# AJAX training backlog (build later — inventory only)

We are **not** authoring full training modules in this sprint. Below is the **detailed list** of what the field system will need so coordinators can produce materials in order.

## Summary

Training must support **~100 organizers** across **wards → neighborhoods → blocks**, with **faith-first outreach**, **petition discipline**, **metered comms** (Twilio/SendGrid), and **initiative-only** messaging (firewall from RedDirt/SOS). Materials should assume **high-school reading level**, heavy visuals, and short video where possible.

---

## 1. Civic & initiative content (public-facing literacy)

1. **At-large vs ward-only in 90 seconds** — script + one diagram; no race framing; economics + accountability.
2. **What signing does / does not do** — legal boundaries; “not a party pledge.”
3. **Petition mechanics** — wet signature rules, notary path, common mistakes (link existing mistakes page).
4. **Ballot initiative timeline** — where AJAX is in the process; what happens after qualification.

## 2. Faith pathway

5. **Pastor conversation guide** — 10-minute agenda; ask for education not endorsement.
6. **Bulletin inserts** — short + long; plain-language; optional QR to `/faith`.
7. **Hosting a signing table at a congregation** — facility respect, volunteer roles, cleanup.
8. **Interfaith etiquette** — denomination differences; no pressure tactics.

## 3. Organizing ladder

9. **Ward captain playbook** — weekly rhythm, data hygiene, reporting signature counts.
10. **Neighborhood lead** — map segmentation; faith institution inventory; recurring touches.
11. **Block captain** — 20-door model; relational texting; deferring legal questions.
12. **Power of 5** — already partially in product; align training copy with dashboard.

## 4. Comms & tools (with cost awareness)

13. **Twilio SMS 101 for captains** — opt-in, local ID, quiet hours, cost per segment; link to campaign manager meter.
14. **SendGrid email 101** — lists, surveys to officials (not voter spam), bounce handling.
15. **Social media roles** — content calendar, boosting boundaries, local newspaper tie-ins.
16. **Earned media** — press release template; who speaks for AJAX.

## 5. Data & compliance

17. **Voter file & crosswalk ethics** — what gets logged; what exports to Peoplebase (scoring only).
18. **Interaction logging SOP** — minimum fields; no sensitive gossip in notes.
19. **FOIA handling** — chunk review workflow once documents land in Supabase.
20. **Finance & grant compliance** — NAACP grant reporting; treasurer approvals; receipt capture.

## 6. Elected officials

21. **Official survey protocol** — single send; follow-up; publishing rules when `publish_ok`.
22. **Research standards for official pages** — news sources, date stamps, correction policy.

## 7. GOTV phase (later)

23. **Phone bank** — script shell tied to ward; initiative-only.
24. **Text bank** — compliance checklist; link to meter.
25. **Canvass** — turf packets; literature; incident escalation.

---

### Delivery formats (when built)

- PDF one-pagers per item above.
- Optional Loom-style short videos (3–6 min).
- In-app tooltips linking from ward dashboard sections.

Track completion in a simple checklist spreadsheet or future `training_module` table.
